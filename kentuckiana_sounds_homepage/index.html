Listen to the new Kentuckiana Sounds series on WFPL and subscribe on iTunes or wherever you get your podcasts!

Choose a pin and press play to start exploring the sounds of Kentuckiana.

Visit the Kentuckiana Sounds collection page at the Internet Archive to view a list of all recordings.

Kentuckiana Sounds is a sound map and collection of audio recordings submitted by the residents of the greater Louisville / Southern Indiana community. The Kentuckiana Sounds series and podcast are produced in association with Louisville Public Media. This project is supported by a 2019-2020 Imagine Greater Louisville 2020 grant from Fund for the Arts and Louisville Metro Government.
